Welcome

Plausible Analytics is a simple, open source, lightweight (< 1 KB) and privacy-friendly alternative to Google Analytics. Plausible is trusted by thousands of paying subscribers to deliver their website and business insights. The full-time team is based in the EU and consists of Uku Taht and Marko Saric. We are completely independent, self-funded and bootstrapped. Read more about us.

This is the Plausible Analytics documentation. Our documentation is a great place to find most answers and make sure that your experience using Plausible Analytics is a great one. Please use the search box in the top right or the navigation menu on the left-hand side to explore what Plausible can do for you and find the answers you're looking for.

Cannot find what you're looking for? Please do contact us so we can update the documentation. Thanks, and enjoy Plausible!
